# Microservices Research

## What Are Microservices?

Microservices divide an application into smaller independent services.

## Netflix Services

- Authentication
- Search
- Recommendations
- Playback
- Billing

## Benefits

- Independent Deployment
- Better Scalability
- Fault Isolation
- Easier Maintenance

## Drawbacks

- Increased Complexity
- Service Communication Challenges

## Key Learning

Microservices enable large-scale systems to evolve more efficiently.
